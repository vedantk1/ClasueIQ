COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Commercial Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on December 1, 2023, between ABC Properties LLC ("Landlord") and XYZ Corporation ("Tenant").

PREMISES: The Landlord agrees to lease to the Tenant the premises located at 123 Business Park Drive, Suite 500, City, State 12345 ("Premises").

TERM: The term of this lease shall be for five (5) years, commencing on January 1, 2024, and ending on December 31, 2028.

RENT: The Tenant agrees to pay a monthly rent of $5,000, due on the first day of each month. Late payments will incur a penalty of $100 per day.

SECURITY DEPOSIT: The Tenant shall provide a security deposit of $10,000 upon signing this agreement.

USE: The Premises shall be used solely for office purposes and related business activities.

MAINTENANCE: The Tenant is responsible for routine maintenance and repairs. The Landlord shall handle structural repairs and major systems.

TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this agreement with 90 days written notice.

LIABILITY: The Landlord shall not be liable for any damages to the Tenant's property or business operations unless caused by gross negligence.

GOVERNING LAW: This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
